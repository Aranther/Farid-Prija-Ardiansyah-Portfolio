001 — Profile

Behind Every Visual, There's a Strategy.

I'm a graphic designer who believes that every design has a purpose. It's not just about making things look good, but also about helping businesses build trust, improving the user experience, and leaving a lasting impression.

Over the past few years, I have helped various businesses in the F&B, retail, healthcare, and UMKM sectors build a consistent visual identity through branding, social media, and digital design.

Every project begins with understanding the business. I then translate those goals into a simple, functional, and characterful design.
